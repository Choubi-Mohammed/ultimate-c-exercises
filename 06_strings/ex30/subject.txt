Exercise 30: URL Parser (Simple)

Practice Problem: Given a simple URL string (e.g., “http://www.example.com/page“), extract and print the protocol (“http”), domain (“www.example.com“), and path (“/page”).

Given:

char url[] = "https://www.google.com/page/c_programming";

Expected Output:

URL: https://www.google.com/search/c_programming
Protocol: https
Domain: www.google.com
Path: /search/c_programming
