Exercise 20: Print Multiplication Tables

Practice Problem: Write a C program to print multiplication tables for numbers 1 to 10, each from ×1 to ×10.

Expected Output:

Table of 1
 1 x  1 =   1
 1 x  2 =   2
 ....
 1 x 10 =  10

Table of 2
 2 x  1 =   2
 2 x  2 =   4
 ...
 2 x 10 =  20
....

Table of 10
10 x  1 =  10
10 x  2 =  20
....
10 x 10 = 100
